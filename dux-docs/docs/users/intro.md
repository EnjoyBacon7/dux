# Welcome to Dux üëã

Dux is not just a job board; it's an AI-powered career assistant designed to bridge the gap between your resume and your dream job. By leveraging official data from **France Travail** and advanced **Artificial Intelligence**, Dux helps you understand exactly how well you match a job description.

## Quick Start

1.  **Create an Account:** Sign up or use **LinkedIn** for one-click registration.
2.  **Upload your CV:** Go to the Home Dashboard and upload your PDF resume.
3.  **Get Scored:** Dux immediately analyzes your CV quality.
4.  **Find Jobs:** Search for offers and use the **" Analyze"** button to get AI feedback.

---

## Home Dashboard & CV Score

Once logged in, Dux analyzes your uploaded CV and provides a **Quality Score** (0-100) based on 5 criteria:
* **Completeness:** Is your profile fully filled out?
* **Experience Quality:** How well are your past roles described?
* **Skill Relevance:** Do your skills match market standards?
* **Impact:** Does your CV demonstrate concrete results?
* **Clarity & Consistency:** Is the format professional?

> **Tip:** Follow the "Recommendations" list below the score chart to improve your ranking!

---

## Intelligent Job Search

Dux connects directly to the **France Travail API** to fetch thousands of real-time job offers.

### Advanced Filters
You can filter jobs precisely using:
* **Keywords** (e.g., "Data Engineer")
* **Location** (Region, Department, or specific Municipality code)
* **Contract Type** (CDI, CDD, Freelance...)
* **Experience Level** & **Minimum Salary**

## AI Match Analysis

*The core feature of Dux.*

When you click on the analyze button, Dux uses AI to compare your specific CV against that specific job offer.

**The Analysis Modal explains:**
1.  **Technical Score:** Do you have the hard skills required?
2.  **Culture Fit:** Does your profile tone match the company culture?
3.  **Verdict:** A direct "Go/No-Go" summary.
4.  **Strengths:** Why you should apply.
5.  **Missing Skills:** What you might need to learn or emphasize in your cover letter.

*Note: The analysis automatically adapts to your selected language (English, Spanish, French, etc.).*
