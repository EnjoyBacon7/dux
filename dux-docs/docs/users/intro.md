# Welcome to Dux

Dux helps you search, rank, and apply to jobs faster.

- Connect your accounts (e.g., LinkedIn) to enrich your profile.
- Upload your CV to improve matching quality.
- Explore AI-ranked job opportunities tailored to your preferences.

## Quick Start

1. Create your account and sign in.
2. Upload a PDF CV.
3. Visit Job Search to browse and apply.

## Privacy

Your data stays yours. See the Privacy Policy in the app for details.
